Frequently Asked Question

What services do you offer?

I specialize in front-end development. I create responsive, user-friendly, and visually appealing websites and landing pages.

What is your approach to responsive design?

I ensure that all my projects are fully responsive, meaning they look and function great on all devices, from desktops to tablets and smartphones. I use mobile-first design principles and thoroughly test across different screen sizes.

How much do you charge for a project?

My rates depend on the scope and complexity of the project. After discussing your requirements, I can provide a detailed quote. Feel free to contact me for a consultation.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline varies based on the project’s size and complexity. A typical small to medium-sized project can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks. I will provide a more accurate timeline after discussing your project in detail.

Do you work with clients globally?

Yes, I work with clients from all over the world. Thanks to modern communication tools, we can easily collaborate remotely, regardless of your location.

Can you help with website design?

While my primary focus is on front-end development, I also have experience in UI/UX design. I can create design mockups and collaborate with designers to ensure the final product meets your aesthetic and functional requirements.

What technologies do you use?

I use a variety of technologies and tools, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, bootstrap, tailwind, daisyUI Git, and more. I continuously update my skills to keep up with the latest trends and best practices in front-end development.

Do you work on-site or remotely?

I am flexible and can work remotely, depending on your needs. I am comfortable collaborating with remote teams using tools like Slack, Zoom, and Trello.

Can you provide examples of your work?

Yes, you can view examples of my previous projects in the Portfolio section of my website.

How do we get started?

To get started, you can reach out to me through the Email address or Whatsapp number on my website. We can schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline.
